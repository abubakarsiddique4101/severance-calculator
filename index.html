Money and financial support

Calculate your severance pay

If you lost your job, your employer may have to pay you money. This tool gives you an estimate.

Who this is for

This tool can help if all of these are true:

  1. You worked for the same employer for at least 2 years (104 weeks).
  2. You were aged 16 to 64 on your last day at work.
  3. You lost your job because of redundancy, a natural disaster, or being kept on lay-off or short-time.

It may not help if:

  1. You were dismissed because of your conduct.
  2. You turned down a similar job from the same employer.
  3. Your job type is excluded.

When to use this tool

You can use it any time. If you want to claim, do it within 12 months of your last day at work.

How to get an estimate

It takes about 3 minutes. We will ask you:

  1. Why you lost your job
  2. How many complete years you worked there
  3. How much you are paid

Start now

After you get your estimate

This is an estimate of the minimum amount. Your contract may give you more. Your employer should pay within 2 months.

If they do not pay, contact the Ministry of Labour.
